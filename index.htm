Welcome to Budgie Network - a Minecraft server network owned and hosted in Australia.
Play Survival, Skyblock, SkyGrid, Creative, or Vanilla game modes.

Start your adventure

The Survival gamemode takes the original Minecraft experience and adds all kinds of progression and challenges.

Additions include:
  • Skill Progression (McMMO)
  • Rank Progression
  • Player Market & Auction House
  • Crates and Minigames
  • Dungeons and unique equips


Claim your land

Unlock unique abilities and items
